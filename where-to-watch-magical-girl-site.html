Where to Watch Magical Girl Site

With a new volume of the manga series on the way, it might be time for anime fans to rewatch Magical Girl Site. The series first aired over three years ago, but if you’re on the hunt now, we can help guide you to the right places. The supernatural series is, indeed, streaming on a major platform, waiting for you to hop in and catch up with the magical Aya Asagiri. So what are you waiting for?

Magical Girl Site is based on Kantarō Satō’s manga of the same name, which follows young Aya as she battles evil middle schoolers and her vicious brother Kaname’s at home abuse. But she finally earns the ability to fight back when some creepy person pops up on her computer screen, granting her magical powers to battle the demons that stalk her in her young life. The anime debuted back in 2018, but new manga are still releasing in the series — the written saga already has 16 volumes, and it’s only been around since 2014!

Wondering where to watch Magical Girl Site? Here’s everything you need to know.

Is Magical Girl Site on Netflix or Crunchyroll?

No, sorry, the anime series isn’t available to stream on Netflix, nor Crunchyroll. But Magical Girl Site is available to stream at another popular streaming service, so keep reading to find out where that may be.

Is Magical Girl Site available to stream? Where to watch Magical Girl Site anime:

Good news: Magical Girl Site is streaming, and if you have the right subscription, you won’t have to pay a penny. Magical Girl Site is currently streaming free for all Prime Video users. Head on over to Amazon and get watching!

How many seasons of Magical Girl Site are there? Will there be more episodes of Magical Girl Site?

There is only one season of Magical Girl Site, and at the moment, it looks like there aren’t any plans for more episodes. In total, there are 12 episodes in the series. There is, however, a new book releasing in English later this year. On November 23, look out for Volume 16 in the manga series.
