Career

On April 24, 2008, KSI launched a YouTube channel called ‘JideJunior.’ According to his website, Weepeler and Hjerpseth were his biggest inspirations for starting his own YouTube career. Following his actions at a Eurogamer event in 2012, he became the center of a controversy. In February 2013, he appeared in GWRomg’s video “KSI VS FIFA / The Record Slam.” He signed a contract with Maker Studios’ sub-network in October 2013.

On August 4, 2017, he tweeted that he was leaving the Sidemen due to disagreements with fellow member Ethan Payne. In 2013, his name was included in the Guinness World Records Gamer’s Edition. As of June 2020, KSI had over 21.5 million subscribers to his main YouTube channel. He became one of the most popular digital media superstars in the UK in his twenties. As an actor, he appeared in the British comedy film Laid in America (2016) and was the subject of a documentary film KSI: Can’t Lose (2018).
