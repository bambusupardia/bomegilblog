What are the steps and ingredients you need to make Kombucha at home?

Let’s see how the Kombucha gets processed to make a bloating drink. I have listed some points that are important to follow to make this drink better and more helpful for the people who are suffering. 

Three steps are important to make this Kombucha.

  • Make SCOBY – it requires 1 to 4 weeks to make mother drink. 
  • The first fermentation takes 6 to 10 days to make the actual Kombucha.
  • Second fermentation – the last process in which 3 to 10 days more are required to make carbonated drinks. 

SCOBY ingredients 

  • 7 Cups of clean water 
  • Half a cup of sugar 
  • 4 bags of black tea 
  • 1 tablespoon loose tea 
  • 1 cup unpasteurized and unflavoured kombucha 
  • Glass or ceramic contained 
  • Tightly woven cloth 
  • Rubberbands
