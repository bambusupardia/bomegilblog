The Charmander & Friends is a Theme Deck from Pokémon Trading Card Game for the Game Boy Color. There are two versions of the Charmander & Friends Deck. The first one, known in Japanese as (Japanese: ヒトカゲとともだちデッキ Hitokage and Friends Deck), is offered to the player by Dr. Mason at the start of the game and is found in the Deck Save machine. It predominantly focuses on , , and type Pokémon. The second one, known in Japanese as (Japanese: ヒトカゲとなかまのデッキ Hitokage and Companions Deck), is available from the Auto Deck Machine and focuses on , , and type Pokémon.
