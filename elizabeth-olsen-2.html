Career

Elizabeth Olsen started her career as a child actress and starred in six of Mary-Kate and Ashley’s productions. She was just four years old when she made her debut but after some years she decided to quit acting due to her sister’s eating disorder in the year 2004. Prior to this incident, she has been featured in several short videos like Our First Video, Olsen Twins Mother’s Day Special, and The Adventures of Mary-Kate & Ashley.

In the year 1994, she has also worked in a television film titled How the West Was Fun and played a cameo role in the movie. After a long break from the industry, she made her comeback in the year 2011 when she made her appearance in the movie titled Martha Marcy May Marlene.

In the same year, she has made her appearance in movies like Silent House and Peace, Love & Misunderstanding. After that, she has been featured in films like Red Lights and Liberal Arts in the year 2012. Following years she made her appearances in popular films like Kill Your Darlings, In Secret, Old Boy, Godzilla, I Saw the Light, Very Good Girls, and several other movies.

She gained worldwide recognition after she joined the cast of Marvel Cinematic Universe and made her appearances in movies like Avengers Infinity War, Avengers End Game, Captain America Civil War, and various other superhero movies.

Along with that she has also worked in some popular television series like Drunk History, Harmon Quest, Sorry for Your Loss, and Wanda Vision.
