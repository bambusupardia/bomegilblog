'American Idol' Top 4 Song Choices Revealed, Including an Iconic Pick for Grace Kinstler

September 06, 2024
post

The top four contestants on ABC’s fourth season of American Idol will be performing on Sunday night and we just found out their song choices!

For the top 4 round, each contestant will perform two solo songs and they will also pair up for duet performances. The solo songs will be their original singles and songs from their personal idols. We can’t wait to hear Grace Kinstler‘s song by her idol!!!

Finneas is the guest mentor this week and the duet performances will feature the contestants performing his songs.

Grace Kintsler

Personal Idol Song: “A Moment Like This” by Kelly Clarkson

Duet Song: “What They Say About Us” with Willie

Winner’s Single Song: “Love Someone”

Chayce Beckham

Personal Idol Song: “Colder Weather” by Zac Brown Band

Duet Song: “Break My Heart Again” with Casey

Winner’s Single Song: “23″

Willie Spence

Personal Idol Song: “Glory” by John Legend and Common

Duet Song: “What They Say About Us” with Grace

Winner’s Single Song: “Never Be Alone”

Casey Bishop

Personal Idol Song: “wish you were gay” by Billie Eilish

Duet Song: “Break My Heart Again” with Chayce

Winner’s Single Song: “Love Me, Leave Me”
