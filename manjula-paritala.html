Career

The actress started her career at an early age, when she was in college she gets an offer for a role in a television show by her father’s friend who is a director. At the start of her career, she has appeared in many Kannada television shows.

After that, the actress shifted her interest towards the Telugu television industry and get her debut chance in the TV serial “Chandramukhi” in 2008. She has appeared in the serial in a lead role along with her husband Nirupam Paritala. She worked in this serial for a long span of five years.

In 2013, she made her debut in the Tamil television industry with Sun TV’s serial “Ponnunjal” as Priya Varun. In this serial, she appeared in a negative role and gained a huge appreciation from the viewers.
